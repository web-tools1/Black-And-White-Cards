Black and White Cards is an online clone of Cards Against Humanity under the Creative Commons BY-NC-SA 2.0 license.

Black and White Cards allows you to play Cards Against Humanity online. You can find open games via the game browser or create a new game to play with your friends.

Please note that game is intended to be played with at least 3 people. Anything less may not produce the desired experience.

Games currently do not end at any point.
